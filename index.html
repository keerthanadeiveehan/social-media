Some types of famous social media platforms are...

  • snapchat
  • facebook

These are just some of the social media platforms. There are thousands more. Social media is used for connecting to people virtually, for entertainment, sharing info, opinions&reviews and many, many more.

Social Media Addiction

Although social media can be fun and used for productive reasons, many people are addicted. If the addictions get worse, it can lead to mental health problems. Many people face depression, suicidal thoughts, bullying, axiety, etc.
